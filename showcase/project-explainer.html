How It Works

3-Stage AI Pipeline

Your voice goes through three AI stages — captured, translated, and spoken back in another language.

🎤 Stage 01 Speech → Text Faster-Whisper
→
🌐 Stage 02 Text → Translation Google Translate
→
🔊 Stage 03 Text → Speech gTTS
🗣 You Speak → 📝 Transcribed → 🔄 Translated → 🎧 You Hear
